Delete Your Mind-Crafter Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Mind-Crafter platform, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at mindcrafter29@gmail.com with the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  2. In the email body, include:
    • Your registered email or username
    • Your role (Student / Faculty / Admin)
    • The reason for deletion (optional)
  3. Our support team will verify your identity and confirm deletion within 5 working days.
  4. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Having trouble?

You can also reach us directly at sudeepwebdev@outlook.com for urgent concerns or data removal issues.
